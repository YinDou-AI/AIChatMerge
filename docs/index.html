Privacy Policy for AIChatMerge

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

Overview

AIChatMerge is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what data the extension collects, how it's used, and your control over it.

In short: AIChatMerge stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal information to third parties or external servers.

What Data We Collect and Store

1. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All of the following data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage and never leaves your device:

  • Prompt Library Data: Prompt titles, content, categories, and tags you create
  • User Preferences and Settings: Theme, enabled AI providers, keyboard shortcuts, language preferences
  • Usage Metadata: Last opened provider, prompt usage counts

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

AIChatMerge does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history
  • IP addresses
  • Analytics or telemetry data
  • AI conversation content
  • Login credentials or API keys

Third-Party Services

AI Provider Websites

AIChatMerge loads AI provider websites inside embedded panels. These providers operate under their own privacy policies. Prompts you send are transmitted to the AI provider you selected.

Permissions Explained

PermissionPurpose

downloadsExport Markdown files and backup JSON files

Your Data Control Rights

  • Export: Settings → Data Management → Export
  • Delete: Settings → Data Management → Reset All Data
  • No Account Required: Works without registration or login
